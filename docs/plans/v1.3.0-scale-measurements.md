## Baseline invalid behavior

Before preflight, requested 8M allocated 7,939,819 particles and repeatedly submitted
invalid `DispatchWorkgroups(124060, 1, 1)` commands against the 65,535 limit. Raw
evidence: `captures/v1.3-baseline-8m-invalid.png.console.txt`.

## Optimization decision

No runtime particle decimation or dispatch tiling ships in v1.3. The valid detailed
cliff row does not isolate render as the sole cost, and render decimation would change
the preserved particle look. Raising the hard ceiling requires coordinated tiled
particle indexing across mark, scatter, G2P/advect, and impulse paths; that is larger
than a safely verified local optimization and includes wall-contact-sensitive G2P
work outside this phase. The shipped measured scale win is preventing impossible
configurations from allocating/submitting invalid work while exposing the exact cliff
and costs.
